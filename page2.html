Stacked Bar Chart

The stacked bar chart shows total revenue by region while also displaying how different products contribute to each region’s total. This makes it easier to see both overall performance and product composition.

Grouped Bar Chart

The grouped bar chart compares units sold across products and regions. It allows side-by-side comparison and makes it easier to identify which products perform better in different regions.
